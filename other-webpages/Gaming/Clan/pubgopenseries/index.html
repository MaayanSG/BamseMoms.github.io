OCE Open Series



Overview

Throwdown is diving head first into the Battle Royale genre with the PUBG OCE Open Series.

The OCE Open Series provides Pro, Semi-Pro and Grass-Roots squads a chance to compete for their share of a $15,000 prize pool in a league spanning 5 weeks.

Format

Stage 1 - Open Qualifier

The Stage 1 Open Qualifier will take place on Thursday nights and will be open to all PUBG players.

Thursday will be an Open Qualifier where the top 18 Squads of the night will qualify for the Closed Qualifier held on the upcoming Saturday.

Stage 1 - Closed Qualifier

The Stage 1 Closed Qualifier will feature the top 18 teams from the Thursday night Open Qualifier. Taking place on Saturday and will be broadcasted live over at Twitch.

Each week the top 4 squads from the Closed Qualifier will qualify for the Stage 2 Grand Final taking place on Saturday October 13th.

Stage 2 - Grand Final

The Stage 1 Grand Final will see the top 16 teams from the previous 4 weeks of the OCE Open Series battle it out for their share of the $15,000 prize pool.
2 additional teams that competed in any of the 4 Closed Qualifiers will be chosen as a Wildcard entry based on kills.
